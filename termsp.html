Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Kora Reporters, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website. We want you to feel confident about how we handle your data, so we've written this policy in simple, easy-to-understand language.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at contactoureditors@gmail.com.

What Information We Collect

When you visit Kora Reporters, we may collect certain information automatically. This helps us understand how people use our website and improve your experience. Here's what we collect:

  • IP Address: Your computer's internet address, which helps us understand where our readers are located
  • Browser Information: The type of browser you use (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) and your device type
  • Pages Visited: Which articles you read and how long you spend on each page
  • Referring Sites: How you found our website (like from Google search or social media)
  • Email Address: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • Country Information: Your country location to help us provide relevant content

How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. We use cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for our website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense and other advertisers to show you relevant ads
About Google AdSense Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website and other sites on the internet. These cookies help Google show you ads that are more relevant to your interests.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not work properly.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We work with Google AdSense and other third-party advertising companies to display ads on our website. Here's how this works:

  • Personalized Advertising: Google uses cookies to show you ads based on your browsing history and interests
  • Non-Personalized Advertising: Some ads are shown based on the content of the page rather than your personal information
  • Ad Performance: Advertisers track how well their ads perform on our site
  • Frequency Capping: Limits how many times you see the same ad

Important: Google and other third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Third-party advertisers may use their own cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your activities on our site and other websites. We do not control these third-party tracking technologies.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect helps us provide a better experience for our readers. Here are the specific ways we use your information:

  • Improve Our Website: Understand which articles are popular and what content our readers enjoy
  • Analytics: Track website performance and identify technical issues
  • Content Personalization: Show you content that might interest you based on your reading history
  • Newsletter Delivery: Send email updates to subscribers who have opted in
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads to support our free content
  • Security: Protect our website from malicious activities

We never use your personal information for purposes other than those described in this policy without your explicit consent.

Data Protection and Your Rights

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Here's our promise to you:

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We want to be absolutely clear: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone. Your data is used only for the purposes described in this policy.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contactoureditors@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Third-Party Links and Content

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which you should review before providing any personal information.

Disclaimer: We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites. When you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

This privacy policy applies only to information collected by Kora Reporters through our website. It does not apply to information collected by third-party websites, even if they are linked from our site.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers as quickly as possible.

We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's internet usage and to help enforce our Privacy Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through any website without their permission.

Your Consent and Changes to This Policy

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our site.

Consent Statement: When you voluntarily provide us with personal information (such as subscribing to our newsletter), you are giving us permission to collect, use, and store that information as described in this Privacy Policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Email: contactoureditors@gmail.com

We value your privacy and will respond to your inquiries as quickly as possible.
