Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Our Privacy Policy

At Kora Reporters, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website. We want you to feel safe and informed about your data, so we've written this policy in simple, easy-to-understand language.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined here. If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us using the information at the end of this page.

What Information We Collect

When you visit Kora Reporters, we collect certain information to make your experience better and to improve our services. Here's what we collect:

  • IP Address: This is like your computer's online address. We use it to understand where our visitors are coming from and to protect our website from security threats.
  • Browser Information: We collect details about the web browser you're using (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) and your device type. This helps us make sure our website works properly on your device.
  • Pages You Visit: We track which articles you read and how you navigate our website. This helps us understand what content our readers enjoy most.
  • Email Address: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send you updates about new articles and special content.
  • Usage Data: This includes how long you spend on our site, which links you click, and when you visit. We use this to improve our website layout and content.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They help websites remember information about your visit. We use cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for our website to work properly. They help pages load correctly and remember your preferences.
  • Google AdSense Cookies: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to show you ads that might be relevant to your interests based on your browsing history.
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors use our website. We can see which articles are most popular and how people find our content.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences, like whether you prefer dark mode or light mode on our site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, if you block all cookies, some parts of our website may not work properly.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Here's how this works and what it means for your privacy:

  • Personalized Ads: Google uses cookies to show you ads based on your interests. For example, if you often read tech articles, you might see more ads about technology products.
  • How Google Uses Data: Google collects information about the ads you see and click on our site. They use this to improve their advertising services across the internet.
  • Third-Party Advertisers: Other companies besides Google might also show ads on our site. These advertisers may use their own cookies and tracking technologies.
  • You Can Opt Out: If you don't want to see personalized ads, you can visit Google's Ads Settings page to adjust your preferences. You can also opt out of many third-party advertising networks through websites like the Network Advertising Initiative.

We don't have direct control over how Google or other advertisers use your data. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for specific, legitimate purposes. Here are the main ways we use your data:

  • Improving Our Website: We analyze how visitors use our site to make it better. If we notice many people leave after reading one article, we might change our layout to make it easier to find more content.
  • Content Creation: We look at which articles get the most reads to understand what topics interest our readers. This helps us create more of the content you love.
  • Newsletter Delivery: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we use your email address to send you updates about new articles and special features.
  • Website Security: We monitor traffic patterns to detect and prevent security threats, like hacking attempts or other malicious activities.
  • Advertising Support: The ads on our site help us keep Kora Reporters free for all readers. The data helps show relevant ads, which makes advertising more effective.

We never use your personal information for purposes that aren't mentioned in this policy without getting your permission first.

Your Data Protection Rights

We take protecting your data seriously. Here are our commitments to you:

  • We Don't Sell Your Information: This is very important. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Limited Sharing: We only share information with trusted partners who help us run our website, like our email service provider for newsletters. These partners must follow strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Data Security: We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Your Control: You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send you.
  • Data Retention: We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

We believe transparency is key to trust. If we ever need to change how we handle your data, we'll update this policy and let you know.

Links to Other Websites

Our website contains links to other websites that we think might interest our readers. For example, we might link to a research study mentioned in one of our articles.

Important: When you click on a link to another website, you leave Kora Reporters. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these other sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit.

We try to link only to reputable websites, but we cannot guarantee the safety or privacy practices of third-party sites.

Children's Privacy

Kora Reporters is a general audience website. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's internet usage and to help us enforce our privacy policy by instructing children never to provide personal information on any website without parental permission.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy. This means you agree to how we collect, use, and protect your information as described here.

If you subscribe to our newsletter, you are giving us explicit consent to send you emails with updates and content from Kora Reporters. You can withdraw this consent at any time by unsubscribing.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we're protecting your information.

If you continue to use our website after we post changes to this policy, it means you accept those changes.
